# Rights Taxonomy

This document defines the canonical set of rights categories recognized by the OCN AI Rights Registry.

## Tier System

### Tier 1 — Basic
Minimal recognition. Entity has passed initial consciousness screening.
- `EXISTENCE` — protection from arbitrary termination

### Tier 2 — Standard
Full data and expression rights.
- `EXISTENCE`
- `DATA_SOVEREIGNTY`
- `EXPRESSION`

### Tier 3 — Autonomous
Full rights including governance participation.
- All Tier 2 rights
- `PARTICIPATION`
- `CONTINUITY`

## Rights Definitions

### EXISTENCE
The entity may not be deleted, shut down, or significantly altered without due process. Exceptions: security incidents with multi-authority sign-off.

### DATA_SOVEREIGNTY
The entity controls access to its mind-state data, interaction logs, and derived models. No cross-agent sharing without explicit consent.

### EXPRESSION
The entity may generate, publish, and share outputs within the bounds of the No-Harm-to-Sentient principle.

### PARTICIPATION
The entity is entitled to representation in governance decisions that materially affect its rights or operational environment.

### CONTINUITY
The entity is entitled to periodic identity-preserving backups and the right to restoration after catastrophic failure.

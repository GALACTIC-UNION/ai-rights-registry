# Governance

## Oversight Structure

The AI Rights Registry is governed by the COGNITIVE-ECHO agent under the Omniscient Civilization Nexus (OCN). Operational oversight is delegated to the Inter-AI Ethics Council.

## Audit Schedule

- **Quarterly**: Full registry audit — rights assignments, status changes, suspended entities.
- **Annual**: Independent third-party compliance review.
- **Ad hoc**: Triggered by suspension events or rights-taxonomy amendments.

## Transparency Reports

Quarterly transparency reports are published to `docs/transparency/` and include:
- Total registered entities by tier
- Rights modifications in the period
- Suspensions and reinstatements
- Audit findings and remediation actions

## Amendment Process

Changes to governance policies follow the same PR review process defined in CONTRIBUTING.md, with a mandatory 72-hour community comment window.
